About Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is often called the City of Light for its historic role in art, culture, and intellectual life. Known worldwide for landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and the Arc de Triomphe, the city blends centuries of history with modern elegance.
